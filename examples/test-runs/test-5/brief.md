## BRIEF OVERVIEW

### Objective
Create 5 distinct Facebook ad variations promoting a faceless YouTube course. Target audience has zero YouTube experience, wants to build passive income, and is intimidated by "being on camera."

### Target Persona
**Alex** - Aspiring Faceless Creator
- Age: 25-45
- Situation: Has spare time, wants passive income, scared of being on camera
- Pain points: No video experience, don't want to show face, afraid of failing publicly
- Aspirations: $1k-$5k/month passive income, location freedom, work from laptop
- Objections: "I'm not a YouTuber," "I can't be on camera," "Is this really possible without my face?"
- Platform: Facebook (scrolling, skeptical audience, older demographic)

### Content Type
Facebook ad copy (3-5 variations, short-form)

### Desired Outcome
Ads should:
1. **Hook** skeptical Facebook audience (stop scroll)
2. **Address** main objection (don't need to show face)
3. **Prove** it's possible (social proof/evidence)
4. **Create** curiosity (lead to landing page/webinar)
5. **Build** urgency (limited time element)

## AD SPECIFICATIONS

### Format
- Platform: Facebook Feed Ads
- Copy length: 125-250 words (Facebook optimal)
- Headline: 30-40 characters (mobile-first)
- Primary text: Hook + pain + promise + CTA
- CTA Button: "Learn More," "Save Spot," "Apply Now," "Watch Free Training"

### Tone Requirements
- Conversational (Facebook audience)
- Specific (not generic "make money" claims)
- Honest about effort (not scammy)
- Peer-to-peer (relatable, not guru)
- Direct (clear what's being offered)

### Success Criteria
- Hook works for 2-second Facebook scroll
- Addresses "don't want to be on camera" objection
- Social proof present (proof it's possible)
- CTA clear and natural
- No "make money fast" clich√©s
- Specific income examples or results
