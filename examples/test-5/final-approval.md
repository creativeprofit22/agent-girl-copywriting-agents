- Deploy all 5 ads to small audience (5-10k people)

- Track CTR, engagement, CPC
- Monitor comments/objections

- Scale highest-performing ads (likely Ad 3 or 5)

- Refine audience targeting based on results

**Phase 3: Scale (Week 5+)**
- Expand to larger audience segments

- Introduce retargeting ads for engaged users
- Rotate fresh creative while keeping copy angles

- Click-through rate (target: 3-6%)
